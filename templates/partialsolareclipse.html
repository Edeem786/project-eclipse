Partial Solar Eclipse

A partial solar eclipse occurs when the Moon partially covers the Sun, creating a unique celestial spectacle. During this event, the Moon only partially obscures the Sun's disk, allowing a portion of sunlight to shine through.
While it's not as dramatic as a total solar eclipse, a partial solar eclipse is still a fascinating sight to behold and is visible from specific regions on Earth during its occurrence.

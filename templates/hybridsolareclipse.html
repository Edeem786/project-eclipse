Hybrid Solar Eclipse

A hybrid solar eclipse is a captivating celestial event that transitions between a total solar eclipse and an annular solar eclipse depending on the observer's location.
Observers at certain locations along the eclipse path may witness a total eclipse, while others may see an annular eclipse. This transition from total to annular or vice versa makes hybrid eclipses a fascinating and unique phenomenon in the world of astronomy.

Partial Lunar Eclipse

A partial lunar eclipse is a celestial event where only a portion of the Moon passes through the Earth's shadow, resulting in a partial darkening of the lunar surface.
Unlike a total lunar eclipse, in a partial lunar eclipse, the entire Moon does not enter the Earth's umbral shadow. Instead, only a part of the Moon's surface is dimmed, creating a striking visual spectacle for observers.
